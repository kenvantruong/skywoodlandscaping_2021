About

  • How long have you been in business?
    Established in 2000. Skywood Landscaping is a full service, urban landscape design/build/and construction. We are based in Northern California. We have been creating outdoor living space ever since.
  • Are you insured and licensed?
    We have all necessary licenses for our area, and we carry insurance for all of our projects. Some larger construction projects require special licenses and certifications. We have trusted members of the landscaping community that we work with for those special cases.
  • What kind of services you provide?
    We specialize in all forms of landscape construction:
    - Re-Landscaping
    - Concrete
    - Pavers, Brick - Block - Flagstone
    - Artificial Turf
    - Retaining Walls
    - Sod, Irrigation, Planting, Mulch
    - Pruning - Topping - Trimming
    - Hillside Clean Up - Hauling
    - Pressure Wash Decks/Driveway
    - Sprinkler Systems
    - Drip Systems
    - Drainage Systems
  • How long does a project usually take?
    It depends on the size of the project. Design process can take 1-3 weeks but once scheduled. We have finish jobs where it'll be less than 4 days. One week. two weeks.
